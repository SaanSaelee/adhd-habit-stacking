Habit Stacking

Thanks to the Reddit community, We have compiled a list of some of the unique ways in which the ADHD community can brute force their brains to help build and maintain better habits.

You are not alone.

Here are some unique strategies used by ADHD brains to make their tooth brushing or hygiene routine less unbearable, more effortless and more fun. These techniques apply to all sorts of tasks, not just brushing your teeth.

"Brush your teeth while showering."
"Make it a ritual. Brush mindfully, slowly, tooth by tooth, section by section, focus on the bristles."
"Get different flavored toothpaste to make it more fun."
"Get active and spontaneous: dance around, hop up and down or stand on one leg.""
"YouTube a video on your iPhone or iPad."
"Turn on your favorite song while brushing."

Experts say it takes approximately 21 days to build a habit and 90 days to keep it.
